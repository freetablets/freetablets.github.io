Privacy

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains the basic privacy posture of this static information site. It is written to be simple, readable, and suitable for a lightweight GitHub Pages deployment.

Information this site may collect

As a static site, this project is designed to avoid unnecessary data collection. Basic analytics, server logs, or contact-form submissions may be used depending on the final deployment setup, but no heavy account system is built into this site.

What collected information may be used for

  • Improving site usability and page performance
  • Reviewing broken links or technical problems
  • Responding to editorial correction requests or user feedback

Third-party links

The site may link to external resources such as official consumer information pages. Those external websites operate under their own terms and privacy practices.

Cookies and analytics

If analytics or advertising tools are added later, this policy should be updated to reflect the actual setup. The current static structure is intentionally minimal.

Policy updates

This policy may be revised if the deployment stack changes, analytics are added, or contact methods are expanded.

Why privacy pages matter

Even simple support properties should explain how they handle data and external links. It strengthens trust and makes the site look complete.

FreeTablets Information Hub

This site is a static informational hub built to explain communication assistance topics, common application friction, and practical verification steps in plain language.

It is not a government agency, not a provider, and not a substitute for official eligibility decisions.
