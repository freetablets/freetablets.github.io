Contact

This page exists to make the site look accountable and reachable. For a support property, visible contact information matters because readers need to know the site is maintained by a real publisher.

General contact purpose

Use the contact channel for editorial corrections, factual clarification requests, broken link notices, and general site feedback. This site does not process official eligibility decisions or provider enrollments.

Important notice

This site is not an official government contact point and does not approve benefits. If a reader needs an official eligibility decision or program assistance, they should use the appropriate public-facing official channels.

Deployment note

Replace the placeholder emails with your real publishing address before you go live. A visible working email is one of the simplest trust upgrades you can make.

Why contact pages matter

Support sites often look weak when they have no visible way to reach the publisher. This page fixes that and completes the trust layer.
